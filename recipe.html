Carrot Cake

This recipe is widely known in the Brazilian home. It is delicious for the afternoon tea or as a birthday cake.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of sunflower oil
  • 3 medium carrots
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 2 and 1/2 cups of wheat flour
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder

Frosting

  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 3 tablespoons of chocolate powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup of milk

Instructions

  1. In a blender, add the carrot, the eggs and the oil, then mix.
  2. Add the sugar and whisk again for 5 minutes.
  3. In a bowl or blender, add the flour and then mix again.
  4. Add the backing powder and mix slowly with a spoon.
  5. Bake in a preheated oven at 180Â° C for approximately 40 minutes.

Instructions Frosting

  1. Pour the butter, chocolate powder, sugar and milk into a bowl, then mix.
  2. Take the mixture to the stove and continue mixing until it has a creamy consistency, then pour the frosting over the cake.
