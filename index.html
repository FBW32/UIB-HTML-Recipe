How to make soft spongy Cake

You need following ingredients for making your cake

  • All purpose Flour 250g
  • Butter 50g
  • Eggs 2 large
  • Sugar 50g
  • Vanille extract 1 spoon
  • Baking soda 1/2 spoon

Instruction

  1. Mix all well together.Transfer the batter in baking tray
  2. Put it in pre heated oven at 150 degree for 25 minutes
  3. Here is your CAKE
