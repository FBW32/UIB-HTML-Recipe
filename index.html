How to make soft spongy Cake

You need following ingredients for making your cake

    1 1 tsp salt 1 cup (240 mL) buttermilk or sour milk* 1 cup (240 mL) strong black coffee (1 cup water + 2 tsp instant espresso powder) 1/2 cup (120 mL) vegetable oil 1 tsp vanilla extract
  1. 2 cups (400 g) light brown sugar
  2. 3/4 cups (220 g) all purpose flour
  3. 3/4 cup (85 g) dark, unsweetened cocoa powder
  4. 2 tsp baking powder
  5. 1 tsp baking soda
  6. Baking powder 8g
  7. 2 eggs

Instruction

  1. In a large bowl, combine the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, soda and salt- whisk until incorporated.
  2. Put it in pre heated oven at 150 degree for 25 minutes
  3. ready
