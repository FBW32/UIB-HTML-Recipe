Microwave Chocolate Mug Cake

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour - 2tbsp
  • Sugar - 1tbsp
  • oil - 1 tbsp
  • baking powder - 1/4 tsp
  • vanilla syrup - 1/4 tsp
  • milk - 1tbsp
  • unsweetend cocoa pwd - 1/2 tbsp

Instructions

  1. Add all the dry ingredients in the cup and mix it well
  2. now add oil, milk and vanilla syrup as mentioned above and mix well until there are no lumps
  3. microwave the cup for 2 min and enjoy your delecious microwave mug cake
